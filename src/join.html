Joining Process

Follow these simple steps to become an active member of your chosen club:

  1. Select a Club: Visit the Categories page to find a club that matches your interest.
  2. Fill Registration: Visit the student services desk or use the online portal link to complete your registration form.
  3. Attend Orientation: Meet the team leads and learn about meeting schedules and club expectations.
  4. Get Involved: Start participating in weekly workshops and events to connect with members.

Important Information

Requirements: Most clubs require a minimum CGPA of 2.5 for executive roles. Regular membership has no CGPA requirement.

Need Help?

Contact the Student Services Office or visit the main office during office hours for assistance with the registration process.
